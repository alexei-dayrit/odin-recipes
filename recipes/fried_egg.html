Fried Egg

Description

A simple, yet amazing egg. It's defined by a nice crispy bottom and a nice runny yolk.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat pan to medium-high. Add oil.
  2. Crack egg into pan, but beware of oil splashes!
  3. Cook egg until crispy on the bototm, and just before yolk becomes cooked.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper. Enjoy!
