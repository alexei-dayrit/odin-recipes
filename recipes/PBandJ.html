Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich


Description

Another simple and classic recipe. It's something you make when you're in a rush or for nostalgia.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter (organic, if possible)
  • Strawberry jam, or a flavor of your choosing

Steps

  1. Toast 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter onto one slice
  3. Spread jelly onto the other slice
  4. Combine both bread slices
  5. Cut sandwich in half and enjoy!
