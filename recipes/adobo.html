Adobo

Description

Filipino chicken adobo is the national dish of the Philippines! It's an easy and delicious dish that will make you crave more. The chicken is flavored with a sweet, savory glaze and some light heat from peppercorns.

Ingredients

Chicken and marinade

  • Chicken thighs or wings
  • Garlic cloves
  • Soy sauce
  • White vinegar
  • Bay leaves

For Cooking

  • Canola oil
  • Onions
  • Brown sugar
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine chicken and marinade. Leave to marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Remove chicken from marinade, but save remaining marinade. Heat pan and sear both sides of chicken.
  3. Set chicken aside after searing.
  4. Saute garlic and onion.
  5. Add remaining marinade after 3-5 minutes, along with water, sugar, and black pepper. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and continue simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add chickken to sauce. DO NOT STIR. Simmer for 25 minutes, turning chicken about halfway through.
  7. Once sauce is thickened, enjoy your adobo with rice!
